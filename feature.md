Note: Core MVP should include user authentication, course catalog, video content delivery, basic progress tracking, payment
processing, and certificate generation. Focus on a single user type (individual learners) and essential learning features before
expanding to enterprise or instructor marketplace features.

👥 User Roles
1. 🎓 Learner (Individual User)
The primary end-user of the platform. Learners browse the course catalog, enroll in courses, consume content, take assessments, and earn certificates. This is the core role the MVP is built around.
Key capabilities:

Register/login and manage their profile
Search, filter, preview, and purchase courses
Stream video lessons and track their own progress
Participate in discussion forums and Q&A
Take quizzes and assessments
Download certificates upon completion
Write reviews and ratings
Save courses to wishlist/favorites


2. 🧑‍🏫 Instructor
Content creators who build and manage courses on the platform. They may be independent educators or part of a marketplace model.
Key capabilities:

Create and manage course content (videos, PDFs, SCORM packages, etc.)
Set course pricing, prerequisites, and descriptions
View student enrollment and progress data
Grade assignments and moderate discussions
Access earnings/payout dashboards (marketplace commission model)


3. 🛠️ Admin (Platform Administrator)
Manages the overall platform, users, content quality, and configuration.
Key capabilities:

Manage all users (bulk import/export, batch enrollment)
Approve or moderate courses and instructors
Configure categories, pricing rules, and platform settings
Access full analytics and reporting dashboards
Handle refunds, payments, and subscriptions
Manage email notifications and system settings


4. 🏢 Organization / Corporate Manager
Represents a company or institution that uses the platform for employee/member training. This role is tied to the Corporate Training Suite and Multi-tenant Architecture features.
Key capabilities:

Manage a team/group of learners under their organization
Assign courses and learning paths to employees
Track compliance and skill matrices across the team
Access organization-level analytics and reports
Manage corporate licenses and seats


5. 🔍 Guest / Visitor (Unauthenticated User)
An anonymous visitor who has not yet registered.
Key capabilities:

Browse the public course catalog
Use search and filtering
Preview course content before purchasing
Register or log in to enroll
